From Our Families to Yours

Our family has been building homes in Southern Indiana since the 1920s. We work with you to make your dream a reality. With three generations of experience and a passion for excellence, you will be cared for and thrilled with your new home.

Wininger Construction’s craftsmanship is all-inclusive. Your home will be brought to life to your specifications using our robust network of suppliers and contractors. Our lasting relationships with artisan installers will allow you to share in our confidence.

Our houses are elegant and modern. Our style is contemporary, and our construction is legendary. Providing you with your new home is always our motivation and our inspiration because family is timeless.

“I love this work, and so does my dad. Now, I love building a nice house, but what feels the best is watching a family settle into a home. We built it. We built it for them.”

Tom Wininger
President & Director of Operations
