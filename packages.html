Our Packages

To make the homebuying process as easy as possible, we offer six floorplans that can be paired with five interior packages and five exterior packages. This flexibility allows you to customize your home to fit your unique style and needs. Each combination is designed to ensure a seamless and aesthetically pleasing final product. Our team is here to guide you through every step of the process, from selecting the perfect floorplan to choosing the ideal finishes. With our comprehensive options and expert support, you’ll find the home of your dreams with ease.

Our Floorplans

Our selection features six exquisite floor plans, including four ranch styles and two townhome styles. The ranch-style homes, designed for single-level living, offer spacious open-concept layouts, large kitchens, and master suites with luxurious en-suite bathrooms. Meanwhile, the townhome styles provide a perfect blend of modern urban living with multi-level designs, featuring expansive living areas, private balconies, and flexible space for home offices or additional bedrooms. Each plan is thoughtfully crafted to cater to diverse lifestyle needs, ensuring comfort and style in every corner.
